Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 19, 2026

Introduction

FACEITracker ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our browser extension.

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Information We Collect

FACEITracker stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • FACEIT player nicknames and IDs that you choose to track
  • Player statistics retrieved from the public FACEIT API
  • Your personal notes about tracked players
  • Blacklist entries you create
  • Extension settings and preferences

How We Use Information

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. We use this information solely to:

  • Display tracked player information
  • Show player statistics and match history
  • Maintain your blacklist
  • Remember your preferences

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do NOT:

  • Send your data to any external servers (except FACEIT's public API for retrieving player stats)
  • Collect personal information
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use cookies or analytics

Third-Party Services

FACEITracker uses the official FACEIT API to retrieve public player statistics. When you use the extension, requests are made to:

  • https://open.faceit.com - Official FACEIT API for player data
  • https://api.faceit.com - FACEIT match data

These requests only include player IDs and are subject to FACEIT's own privacy policy. All data retrieved is publicly available on faceit.com.

Data Security

Your data is stored securely in your browser's local storage. Only you have access to this data through the extension. We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • You can delete any tracked player at any time
  • You can clear your blacklist entries
  • You can modify or delete your notes
  • You can uninstall the extension to remove all stored data

Children's Privacy

FACEITracker does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The extension is designed for users of the FACEIT platform, which has its own age requirements.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the extension's update notes and on this page. The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised accordingly.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through:

  • Extension's support page
  • GitHub repository issues
  • Browser extension store reviews

Consent

By installing and using FACEITracker, you consent to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the extension.
