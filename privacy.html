Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 27, 2026

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Introduction

FACEITracker is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our browser extension.

2. Information We Collect

FACEITracker stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • FACEIT player nicknames and IDs that you choose to track
  • Player statistics retrieved from the public FACEIT API
  • Your personal notes about tracked players
  • Blacklist entries you create
  • Extension settings and preferences

3. How We Use Information

All data is stored locally in your browser. We use this information solely to:

  • Display tracked player information
  • Show player statistics and match history
  • Maintain your blacklist
  • Remember your preferences

4. Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do NOT:

  • Send your data to any external servers (except FACEIT's public API)
  • Collect personal information
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use cookies or analytics

5. Third-Party Services

FACEITracker uses the official FACEIT API to retrieve public player statistics. All data retrieved is publicly available on faceit.com.

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • You can delete any tracked player at any time
  • You can clear your blacklist entries
  • You can modify or delete your notes
  • You can uninstall the extension to remove all stored data

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through:

  • GitHub Issues: github.com/BambooFury/faceit-tracker/issues

Your Consent: By installing and using FACEITracker, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
