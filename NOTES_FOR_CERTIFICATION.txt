HOW TO TEST THE EXTENSION:

1. Install the extension in Microsoft Edge.

2. Click the FACEITracker icon in the browser toolbar (orange icon with "FT").

3. A popup window will open automatically.

4. In the popup, you will see a search box that says "Enter nickname".

5. Type one of these player names:
   - s1mple
   - NiKo
   - donk
   - m0NESY

6. Click the "Add" button.

7. Wait 3-5 seconds. The player will appear with their photo, statistics, and match history.

8. You can add more players by repeating steps 5-7.

9. To test the blacklist feature:
   - Click the second icon in the left sidebar (circle with a line).
   - Enter any player name.
   - Click "Add to Blacklist".
   - The player will appear in the blacklist.

10. To test settings:
    - Click the gear icon in the top right.
    - You can change the language between English and Russian.

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- No FACEIT account is required to test the extension.
- No login or registration is needed.
- The extension works immediately after installation.
- All player data is public and fetched from FACEIT's official API.
- The extension stores data locally in the browser only.

TEST PLAYERS:
The players listed above (s1mple, NiKo, donk, m0NESY) are real professional CS2 players with public profiles on FACEIT. Their data is publicly available and can be viewed by anyone.
